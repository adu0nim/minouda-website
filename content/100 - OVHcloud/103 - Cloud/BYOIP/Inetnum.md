**Topic** : #byoip 

#### Objet inetnum complet sinon KO
Info salon BYOIP

The customer is (was) not a direct RIPE member, he is what RIPE calls an "End User", and he was assigned directly a /22 by RIPE but with another company - a RIPE member - being the sponsor. As the customer is only designed as an "Assigned PI", he is not allowed by RIPE to split his /22 into several /24 inetnum objects. Hence, he cannot do what he wants, because OVH only allows import of his full inetnum object, being the /22.
